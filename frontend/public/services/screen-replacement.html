Professional Screen Replacement in the Hills District

A cracked or damaged screen doesn't mean you need a new laptop. Our technicians can replace your screen with a quality panel, often on the same day.

We stock screens for popular laptop models and can source parts for less common brands. All our replacement screens are quality tested and come with a warranty.

Screen Problems We Fix

  • Cracked screens - Physical damage from drops or impacts
  • Lines on display - Horizontal or vertical lines across screen
  • Dead pixels - Black or stuck pixels on the display
  • Flickering screen - Intermittent display issues
  • Dim or dark display - Backlight failure
  • Discoloration - Color distortion or patches

Brands We Service

We replace screens for all major laptop brands including HP, Dell, Lenovo, ASUS, Acer, Toshiba, Samsung, MSI, and Apple MacBooks (including Retina displays).

Screen Replacement Process

  1. Bring in your laptop for free assessment
  2. We identify the exact screen model needed
  3. Receive upfront pricing with no hidden fees
  4. Screen replaced by certified technician
  5. Full testing before return to you
