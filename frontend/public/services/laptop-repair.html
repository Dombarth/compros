Expert Laptop Repair in the Hills District

Is your laptop running slow, overheating, or showing a cracked screen? Our certified technicians specialise in repairing all laptop brands including HP, Dell, Lenovo, ASUS, Acer, Toshiba, and Apple MacBooks.

We understand how essential your laptop is for work, study, and staying connected. That's why we offer fast turnaround times with most repairs completed within 24-48 hours. For urgent cases, ask about our same-day express service.

Common Laptop Problems We Fix

  • Cracked or damaged screens - LCD/LED replacement with quality parts
  • Battery not holding charge - Genuine and compatible battery replacement
  • Overheating and fan noise - Thermal paste replacement and fan cleaning
  • Keyboard not working - Key replacement or full keyboard swap
  • Laptop won't turn on - Power jack, motherboard, and DC jack repairs
  • Slow performance - SSD upgrades, RAM installation, and optimisation
  • Virus and malware infections - Complete system cleanup and protection
  • Broken hinges - Hinge repair and replacement
