Professional Data Recovery in the Hills District

Losing precious photos, important documents, or critical business files is devastating. But don't give up hope - in many cases, we can recover your data even when it seems lost forever.

Our data recovery specialists use professional tools and techniques to retrieve files from damaged, corrupted, or failed storage devices. We handle everything from accidental deletions to complete drive failures.

Storage Devices We Recover From

  • Hard Drives (HDD) - Desktop and laptop mechanical drives
  • Solid State Drives (SSD) - Modern flash-based storage
  • USB Flash Drives - Thumb drives and portable storage
  • Memory Cards - SD cards, microSD, CompactFlash
  • External Drives - Portable hard drives and backup drives
  • RAID Arrays - Server and NAS storage systems

Data Loss Situations We Handle

  • Accidentally deleted files or formatted drives
  • Drive not recognized by computer
  • Clicking or grinding noises from drive
  • Water or physical damage
  • Virus or ransomware encryption
  • Corrupted file system or partition

Important: What NOT to Do

If your drive is making unusual noises or has physical damage, stop using it immediately. Continued use can cause permanent data loss. Contact us right away for professional assessment.
