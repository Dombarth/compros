Computer Hardware Upgrades in the Hills District

Is your computer feeling sluggish? Before you buy a new one, consider an upgrade. Strategic hardware upgrades can dramatically improve your computer's performance at a fraction of the cost of replacement.

Our technicians will assess your system and recommend the most cost-effective upgrades to meet your needs, whether you're a home user, student, or business.

Popular Upgrade Options

  • SSD Upgrade - Replace your slow hard drive with a fast SSD. See boot times drop from minutes to seconds. The single best upgrade for most computers.
  • RAM Upgrade - More memory means better multitasking. Essential for running multiple programs or browser tabs without slowdown.
  • Graphics Card - Better gaming, video editing, and display performance. Multiple monitor support.
  • Storage Expansion - Add more hard drive or SSD space for files, photos, and videos.
  • WiFi Card Upgrade - Faster, more reliable wireless connectivity.

Benefits of Upgrading vs. Replacing

  • Save money - upgrades cost a fraction of a new computer
  • Keep your files and programs - no need to set up from scratch
  • Reduce e-waste - better for the environment
  • Get years more life from your current machine
